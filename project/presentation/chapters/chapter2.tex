  {\color{solutiongreen}\bfseries\scriptsize Core Design Decision --- Claim Check \& Persistent Volumes (PVC):}\\
  \vspace{3pt}
  \raggedright\tiny
  Images are saved directly to \textbf{MinIO}. Only a lightweight URL-reference is passed to RabbitMQ --- not the file itself. 
  Saving to MinIO acts as a trigger for the serverless thumbnail generation. Furthermore, PVCs decouple storage from pods, ensuring data survives restarts.
